Diving Center

Diving Center for all ages

Our Oceanarium provides a wonderful opportunity to learn diving and opens the way to the unique underwater world! After completing scuba diving training at our dive center, you will literally fly underwater! Preparation will take you a little time and will not require much effort. Upon completion of the course, you will receive an international certificate that gives you the right to dive in any country in the world. In our dive center we train divers according to the most popular international training systems in the world: NDL, CMAS, PADI.
